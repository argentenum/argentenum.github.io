I am a [Professor](http://hss.iitd.ac.in/faculty/arjun-ghosh) at the [Department of Humanities and Social Sciences](http://hss.iitd.ac.in/), [Indian Institute of Technology Delhi](http://www.iitd.ac.in/). I was formerly a Fellow at the Indian Institute of Advanced Study, Shimla.

I am the author of [A History of the Jana Natya Manch: Plays For the People](https://arjun.hcommons.org/publications/books/plays-for-the-people/), [Freedom from Profit: Eschewing Copyright in Resistance Art](https://arjun.hcommons.org/publications/books/freedom-from-profit/), and an annotated translation of Bijon Bhattacharya's *Nabanna* with a critical introduction.

I am President of the [Digital Humanities Alliance for Research and Teaching Innovations (DHARTI)](https://dhdharti.in/about/) and a member of the [Voyant Consortium](https://voyant-tools.info/people/).

My research interests include Digital Humanities, Performance and Theatre Studies, Authorship, Copyright and Intellectual Property, and Political Communication.

Prospective applicants for the PhD programme should check [this page](https://argentenum.github.io/people/) for details.
